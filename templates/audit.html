How Federated Learning Works

  1. 1 Each hospital trains a local model on its own patient data
  2. 2 Only model weight hashes are recorded on the blockchain (not raw data)
  3. 3 FedAvg aggregates all hospitals into one global model
  4. 4 Patients get AI risk predictions from the global model
